About Us

Veggie Green

Welcome to Veggie Green. Offering Traditional Dishes of the highest quality.

On the first floor of Jaswant Tuli Mall, You will find a cozy restaurant with classic interior, dominant with calm and warm colors. The restaurant could serve up to 200 persons at the same time. In the evening you are welcome to enjoy candle light and calm music. Evening menu will surprise you with low prices and variety of Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian dishes of various Cruzine. The kitchen chef cooks only from fresh and carefully selected products.We are ready to arrange you an unforgettable celebration, restaurant staff will take care about all the smallest details: decorate the hall, arrange musicians, agree the celebration menu with the customer, will make all kinds of world cuisine dishes.
